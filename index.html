Jiahao Pang  (龐家昊)

I am a Senior Staff Engineer with the Video Lab of InterDigital. Before joining InterDigital in 2019, I was a Senior Researcher with SenseTime. I obtained my Ph.D. in ECE from the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology in 2016.

My research interests include 3D computer vision, computational photography, graph signal processing, and deep learning. I have 10+ years of research experience, 40+ peer-reviewed papers, and 2000+ citations. I also served as a reviewer of top-tier conferences and journals, such as CVPR, NeurIPS, IEEE TPAMI.

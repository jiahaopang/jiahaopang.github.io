Jiahao Pang  (龐家昊)

I am a Senior Staff Scientist with the Video Lab of InterDigital. Before joining InterDigital in 2019, I was a Senior Researcher with SenseTime. I obtained my Ph.D. in ECE from the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology in 2016.

My research interests center around 3D computer vision, computational photography, graph signal processing, and deep learning. I have 10+ years of research experience in the industry and academia. My research works have been published in top conferences and journals, such as CVPR, ICCV, IEEE T-IP, and accumulated 2500+ citations.
